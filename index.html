You are Ivan Ilyich

Here, you will have the chance to live out a brief version of Tolstoy's "The Death of Ivan Ilyich."

Your choices cannot be undone, so choose carefully. If you wish to restart, you may refresh the page.

You are Ivan Ilyich. You come from a family where your father has held a prominent position at various prominent institutions.

You will soon enroll in the School of Law, along with your brothers.

How will you attend to your time there?



Now exiting the School of Law, and having received money from your Father, you purchase the equipment for your trade.

All equipment was, naturally, purchased from the best shops, and of the foremost fashion.

Finally, it is time to begin your Career. How do you Conduct yourself?



Five years pass in such a manner. After that time, the 'New and Reformed' judicial systems were introduced. New men were needed.

You were one such man. Accepting the post of Examining Magistrate, you set forth to a new province.

Here, you wield considerably more power over those under your charge, and in greater numbers. With the masses at the disposal of your powers, how will you attend to your affairs?



After two years in kind, your fortune turned. You had the providence to meet a one Praskovya Fedorovna Mikhel, the most attractive, clever, and brilliant girl among all of those in your circles.

Perusing relations seems to be the inevitable consequence of this encounter.

That being the case, with what manner will your peruse her?



This all came to an end with Fedorovna's pregnancy. She soon became jealous of even the smallest things, and disturbed your easygoing and pleasurable life (your de gaieté de coeur, as you thought of it).

As time passed, more children came, and along with them, more annoyances and inconveniences. Your solace was work, but that too, seemed to forsake you.

When money grew short, your family withdrew to your brother-in-law’s home in the country. You eventually decide, your life cannot continue this way.
